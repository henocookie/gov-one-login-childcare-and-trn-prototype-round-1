Are you sure you want to delete your GOV.UK One Login

This will delete your GOV.UK One Login account.

It will not delete any other government accounts you may have, such as Universal Credit.

What happens after you delete your GOV.UK One Login

Your GOV.UK One Login will not be permanently deleted straight away.

You can change your mind within 14 days - you’ll get an email explaining what to do.

After 14 days:

  • the sign in details for your GOV.UK One Login will be permanently deleted
  • you’ll no longer be able to sign in to services you’ve used with your GOV.UK One Login
  • we will notify all the services you’ve used with your GOV.UK One Login - they may contact you separately about the information they hold about you
Services you’ve used with your GOV.UK One Login
  • Apply for a passport
  • Personal tax account
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Teacher services
  • The childcare service
